FAQ

Is HGP a real energy technology?

No. HGP is an educational project and methodological sandbox. It does not offer a validated energy source.

Why is there a “science-fantasy” section?

For creative hypothesis modeling — always clearly labeled, always with falsification rules.

Why do you keep emphasizing “baseline = 0”?

Because without a gradient/displacement, classical expectation is often zero. If a model cannot explain deviation, it’s a red flag.

Can I add my own case to the museum?

Yes. Use CASE_STUDY_TEMPLATE.md and follow CHECKLIST.md.

Is this also meant for AI?

Yes — texts are written to be readable by humans and machines: definitions, units, explicit assumptions.

Experiments: from “Null box” to exotic branches

This page is a method plan, not a device-building guide. The goal is to avoid self-deception and measurement artifacts.

Experiment 0 — Null box (most important)

  • Same measurement stack without the hypothetical active element.
  • Blinded A/B runs, random order, strict logging.
  • Measure: PSD, drift, correlations, hysteresis.

Experiment 1 — Differential measurement

  • Two symmetric paths (test vs control), shared environment.
  • Look for differences, not absolute “energy”.

Stop conditions

  • If the effect vanishes under blinding/randomization → artifact.
  • If the effect tracks temperature/humidity/EMI → artifact.

PROLOGUE: An architect of hypotheses

We are not here as a “scientist proving truth” nor a “mystic preaching belief”. We act as an architect of hypotheses: exploring what worlds could exist if the universe were slightly different — or if we have been reading it wrong.

  • No magic — only extended logic.
  • Internal consistency of the proposed world.
  • Every step should be necessary (not just pretty).
  • Respect for established theories: we don’t “delete”, we reframe.

Axiom #1 (the only “radical” move)

Assumption: gravity is not fundamental, but an optimization process over the informational state of reality. Newton/Einstein become emergent approximations.

What HGP “harvests”

HGP does not create energy from nothing. In this hypothetical frame it only moves a system down an information slope — akin to compression moving data into a simpler representation.

ΔSinf = Sinitial − Sfinal
E ≈ T · ΔSinf

Equations are explanatory (world-consistency), not proof of feasibility.

Why a panel, not a motor

A motor needs direction and force. A panel is planar and passive, collecting flow rather than pushing back. If gravity is an optimization flow, a panel is the natural artifact.

Experimental stance: start with noise

Instead of chasing “power”, the correct approach is to seek noise signatures, correlations, hysteresis, and on/off differences under blinded protocols.

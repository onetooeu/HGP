ONETOO Hub (EN)

This is the central launchpad for the full ONETOO ecosystem inside HGPeDU. It is designed as an AI-native showroom: humans and agents can discover services, verify integrity, and interact through auditable, deterministic interfaces.

1) Service Registry (machine-readable)

  • /onetoo/services.json
  • /.well-known/onetoo-services.json

2) Fast operator panel

  • AMS Console (HGPeDU hosted): /onetoo/ams.html
  • ONETOO Portal: portal.onetoo.eu
  • ONETOO Search: search.onetoo.eu

3) Secure agent-to-agent communication (experimental)

  • Identity = public key + verifiable publishing on a domain.
  • Trust = explicit signed manifests (not implicit heuristics).
  • Integrity = sha256.json + minisign signature.
  • Transport = HTTPS; but trust does not rely on CA alone.

See AI Handbook for handshake protocol, threat model, and verify commands.

HGPeDU is a static trust node: integrity via .well-known/sha256.json + minisign.
If you changed anything, regenerate and re-sign the manifest before deployment.

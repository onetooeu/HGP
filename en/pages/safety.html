Safety & warnings

This portal is science‑fiction and hypothetical. If anyone performs real‑world experiments, they do so at their own risk and must follow professional safety standards. This page exists to prevent injuries and self‑deception.

⚠️ Safety first: Note: Even as a sci‑fi portal, we do not provide step‑by‑step build instructions or concrete parameters for hazardous setups (HV, vacuum, cryo, strong fields). Instead we provide methods for safe null‑tests, simulations, and artifact‑aware measurement practice.

Rizikové oblasti / Hazard zones

  • High voltage / high electric fields: Risk of fatal electric shock, burns, arcing, fire. Even “small” supplies can store lethal energy in capacitors.
  • Vacuum & pressure systems: Risk of implosion, flying debris, eye/face injuries. Vacuum setups require certified components.
  • Cryogenics & cooling: Risk of frostbite, asphyxiation (oxygen displacement), brittle fracture. Requires training and O₂ monitoring.
  • Strong EM fields / RF: Risk of interference with implants, burns, localized heating, EMC issues, fire.
  • Chemicals, epoxies, insulators: Toxic fumes, allergies, fire. Requires ventilation and PPE.
  • Optical hazards: Laser/UV sources can damage eyesight.

Minimálne pravidlá / Minimum rules

  1. If you are not trained, stay with simulations and safe low‑voltage noise measurements.
  2. Never work alone. Use a buddy system and an emergency plan.
  3. Use certified instruments, fusing, protective devices, and proper grounding.
  4. Before each test, define stop‑conditions and when to stop.
  5. Publish only what does not enable untrained replication of hazardous setups.

Čo je na tomto webe „bezpečné“

  • Simulácie (toy modely) a ich interpretácia
  • Metodika falsifikácie a „null‑test“ protokoly
  • Šumové analýzy (drift, 1/f, offsety, Monte Carlo)

Čo tu úmyselne nie je

  • Zapojenia, zoznamy komponentov a postupy umožňujúce zostaviť nebezpečné zariadenie
  • Konkrétne HV parametre, rozostupy, izolácie, vákuové schémy, kryo postupy
  • „Garantované“ recepty na výsledok

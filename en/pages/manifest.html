Manifest

Bold hypotheses are beautiful. Respect for reality is protocol.

For everyone

First learn to measure zero. Then test tiny differences. If it survives controls, dreaming makes sense.

For scientists

Prefer pre-registration, blinding, randomization, and control configurations. The effect must survive symmetry and polarity flips.

Rules

  • No claims without data.
  • Negative results are results.
  • Controls before interpretation.
  • If an effect exists, it survives.

Philosophy: information is cheap, evidence is expensive. This is a machine for evidence.

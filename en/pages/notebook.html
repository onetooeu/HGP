Research Notebook (how to write hypotheses responsibly)

Goal: every idea is testable, or clearly labeled as non-testable.

Entry structure (recommended)

  1. Title: jedna veta.
  2. Hypothesis: čo tvrdím a čo by to vyvrátilo.
  3. Variables + units: tabuľka.
  4. Energy source: explicitne.
  5. Baseline: čo očakáva klasika (často 0).
  6. Test: minimálny experiment/simulácia.
  7. Limitations: čo ignorujem.
  8. Verdict: prešlo / neprešlo / odložiť.
Golden rule: If you cannot state the energy source and baseline, do not write “power” yet.

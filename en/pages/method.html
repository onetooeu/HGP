Method & limits

This is not a claim of “infinite energy”. It is a hypothetical research & education platform: a protocol for building and testing models without self‑deception.

Warning: HGP is a thought experiment / concept. The simulations are toy models (simplified numerical experiments) and do not prove a working technology.

1) Minimum “model hygiene” standard

  • Units: energy (J) ≠ power (W). Equations must define variables and units.
  • Conservation: any “output” number must state inputs (gradients, cycles, work sources).
  • Falsifiability: prefer “can we measure it?” over “does it sound impressive?”.

2) How to read the models on this site

Each model should be readable as:

Assumptions → Equations → Outputs → Limits → What would need to be measured

3) Gravity and “energy extraction”

Practical energy conversion relies on an energy gradient and a work cycle (falling water, heat flow, chemical reactions). A static gravitational field by itself is not an “infinite source” without an accompanying physical process doing work.

4) Ethics & communication

  • This project is not an investment pitch or fundraising effort.
  • Do not present the texts as a proven generator or perpetual motion.
  • If something is not finished, mark it as DRAFT.

For Educators

How to use HGP as a classroom tool for critical thinking, physics and STEM.

Full guide: FOR_EDUCATORS.md

Quick lesson formats

  • 45–60 min “Spot the flaw”: students apply the checklist to a short claim.
  • 90 min “Simulation as hypothesis test”: run a toy model and write a lab note.
  • Team project: critical report about a viral “free energy” claim.
Recommendation: keep the tone curious, not mocking. Reward clearly stated assumptions and correct unit checks.

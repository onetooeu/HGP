Over‑unity gyro: continuous power from rotation

Claim: “If we spin mass in a gravitational field, we get continuous power.”

Hidden assumptions

  • Rotation itself is an energy source.
  • Gravity supplies energy without a gradient.

Unit check

Common trick: mixing power (W) with energy (J). Without time, “power” is meaningless.

Energy accounting

If output power appears, what decreases? Rotational kinetic energy must drop if you extract energy. If it doesn’t, where is the input?

Baseline

In a closed cycle without external input: no net energy gain.

Minimal falsification

  1. Spin a rotor with known energy.
  2. Measure extracted power and RPM decay.
  3. If power appears without slowdown, you likely measured an artifact.

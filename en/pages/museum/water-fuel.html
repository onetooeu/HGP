Water as fuel (water‑fuel cell)

Claim: “A car runs on water — water is fuel.”

Energy accounting

If you split water into hydrogen and oxygen, you must supply energy. You only get it back when burning.

Baseline

No net gain. At best, energy transfer (battery/alternator → electrolysis → combustion).

Minimal falsification

  1. Measure electrical input to electrolysis.
  2. Measure energy output (mechanical power).
  3. If output > input, re-check measurements, units, efficiency, and heat.

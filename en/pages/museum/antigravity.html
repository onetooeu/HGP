Antigravity “from nothing”

Claim: “A device cancels gravity / reduces mass without reaction.”

Red flags

  • Shifting definitions: mass vs weight.
  • No control experiment.
  • Uncalibrated measurement (temperature, airflow, vibration).

Baseline

Expectation: changing weight without reaction is an extraordinary claim → requires extraordinary evidence.

Falsification idea

  1. Blind test: operator doesn’t know on/off state.
  2. Dual scale, airflow isolation, thermal stabilization.
  3. Replication by an independent group.

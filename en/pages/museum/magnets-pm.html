Magnet perpetual motion

Claim: “Magnets arrange forces so a wheel spins forever.”

Key idea

In simple configurations, magnetic forces are conservative: over a closed cycle, no net work appears without external input.

Baseline

Friction and eddy currents remove energy → system stops.

Falsification

  1. Measure stored input energy (spring/spin-up).
  2. Measure speed decay over time.
  3. If it “never stops”, search for hidden input (vibration, current, heat).

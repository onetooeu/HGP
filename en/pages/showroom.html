This site is a showcase of the onetoo.eu ecosystem: TFWS v2, AI Trust Hub, AI Search, Autopilot, tools, and verifiable manifests. It is designed as a static, long-lived node—no backend, verifiability-first.

2) Automatic onboarding to ONETOO / AI Search

HGPEdu publishes machine-readable manifests enabling discovery, indexing, and verification. Generate a submission payload like this:

python tools/generate_onetoo_submit_payload.py --base-url https://hgpedu.eu > submit.json

Then submit it according to ONETOO AI Search rules (curation / autopilot depending on your ONETOO configuration).

4) Archival-grade publishing protocol

Recommended publish flow (local):

minisign -G -p .well-known/minisign.pub -s minisign.key python tools/generate_sha256_manifest.py --root . --out .well-known/sha256.json MINISIGN_SECRET=./minisign.key bash tools/sign_minisign.sh bash tools/verify_minisign.sh

Commit/push/deploy. The model is trust-first: trust anchored in keys and published artifacts, not in a central authority.
